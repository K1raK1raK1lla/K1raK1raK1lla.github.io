December 22nd 2024

Graduation

Hello once again. Its been a long month and a half since I last posted anything here.

As you can tell from the title, I recently graduated from univeristy.

I have a lot of thoughts and feelings about it right now and its actually kind of insane.

I had begun the semester under the impression that this was my second to last semester, but funnily enough,

I had actually read the credit requirements wrong. I found this out halfway through the semester and had to

quickly find out what I needed to do in order to ensure I could graduate. Thankfully, not too much. Just filling

out some forms and attending a few events. It was very surreal and I've still not fully realized it.

I am done with formal schooling. I am finished and will (HOPEFULLY) now enter the work force.

During my high school graduation, I wasnt all that emotional.

During my commencement ceremony for uni graduation, I found it really hard not to shed a tear. It got me

fairly emotional in a way I hadn't thought possible. I am glad to be done with formal education and free

to follow my own path, but there are definitely some things I'm going to miss.

On the flipside, there are several things I will certainly not miss. Too many to list, in fact.

I do not have a job lined up, but I have a plan in place and have faith in that plan.

I am proud of what I have accomplished in life, and hope to accomplish even more.

AND WITH THAT, CONCLUDES THIS KIRAPOST

RANDOM SKETCH

Sincerely, Kira ❤️
